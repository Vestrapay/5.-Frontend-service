This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access or use our services (the “Services”).

By using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

When you use our Services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Registration Information: When you create an account with VestraPay, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, business details, and payment information.

  • Device Information: We collect data on the devices you use to access our Services, such as IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you interact with our Services, including the pages you visit, links clicked, and actions taken within the platform.

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide and operate our payment gateway services.
  • Process and manage payment transactions.
  • Verify your identity to comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations.
  • Communicate with you about your account, transactions, and customer service requests.
